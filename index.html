Common Questions

How does pricing work?

Pricing is based on the volume your items take up in our trucks. We quote before we start, so there are zero surprises.

Do I need to move anything?

Nope. Point to what goes and we’ll safely remove it from wherever it is—attics, basements, backyards, or job sites.

What items can't you take?

We can’t haul hazardous materials like paint, oil, chemicals, or propane. We’re happy to recommend proper disposal resources.
